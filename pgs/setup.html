Difficulty Tier

Determines the average difficulty of each question.

* Selecting Randomized will choose a random difficulty tier upon start.

Question Category

Determines what topics questions will cover.

* Selecting Randomized will choose a random category every question.

Question Count

Determines the total number of questions, between 10 and 30 questions.

* Selecting Randomized will choose a random question count upon start.

Time Limit

Determines the amount of time allotted per question, if any.

* Selecting Randomized will choose a random time limit upon start.
